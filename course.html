Courses We Offer

We provide different courses in different fields like Bachlor's degree Master's degree etc.

B.TECH

B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering program at Xenon Institute attracts talented undergraduates who are willing to pursue a promising career in Computer Engineering. The industry-aligned curriculum is designed to assimilate core computing theoretical foundations along with coding skills.

Pharmacy

The diploma in pharmacy at Xenon Institute encompasses basic pharmacy education entailing the concepts of biochemistry, pharmacology, and toxicology. Also, it covers the application of chemistry in the pharmaceutical industry. The industry aligned curriculum is designed to let students learn about diverse chemical salts, their application, and their usages in medicine.

BBA

BBA Entrepreneurship and Innovation is a three-year undergraduate course that provides in-depth knowledge of financial planning, marketing strategy, and business practices for starting a business. It is an ideal course for candidates who have business acumen, innovation skills, and a desire to turn innovations into economically beneficial goods/services.
